Khmer Beef Lok Lak

Go Back Home

Description:

One of the most simple yet delicious recipes you can make at home right now! A dish I hold true to my heart. Well seasoned beef served on top of a variety of vegetables (Typically: Chopped Onions, Tomatoes, Cucumber, and Lettuce). Best served with a simple dipping sauce consisting of four simple ingredients: Lemon Juice, Fish Sauce, Ground Black Pepper, and Water. This dish will certainly make your mouth water. The nature of Lok Lak allow you to switch up indgredients to your liking. Don't like beef? Try it with chicken, pork or any other alternatives! Same with the vegetables!

Ingredients

  • 300g beef steaks sliced (best you can afford)
  • 1/4 cup light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato sauce (ketchup)
  • 3 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

To Serve

  • 2 small tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and finely sliced
  • 1/2 small red or white onion, finely sliced
  • lettuce, iceberg or romaine leaf

Dipping Sauce

  • Juice of a lime
  • 2 teaspoons fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt or fish sauce (fish sauce recommended)
  • 1 tablespoon water

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix beef with soy sauce, oyster sauce, tomato sauce, sugar, fish sauce and ground black pepper and garlic. Marinate for atleast 20 minutes or up to one hour.
  2. Make dipping sauce, combine ingredients in a small bowl-set aside
  3. place the lettuce leaves, onions, cucumber, and tomatoes on a seperate platter
  4. Heat oil in a wok over a high heat and stir-fry beef until cooked. Divide between the two dinner plates.
